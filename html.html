About Us

Access to safe, reliable, and affordable transportation remains a persistent challenge in Lesotho, especially in urban centers like Maseru and remote rural areas. Public transport options are often inconsistent, informal, and lack digital integration.

Catch-a-Ride offers a dependable, transparent, and user-friendly ride-hailing service that addresses transportation inefficiencies and creates new economic opportunities for drivers.

How It Works

For Riders

1. Request a Ride

Download the app or use SMS service. Enter your destination and request a ride or delivery instantly.

2. Track & Travel

Track your driver’s arrival in real-time and enjoy a safe, efficient journey.

3. Pay & Rate

Pay securely and provide feedback after your trip to maintain high service quality.

For Drivers

1. Sign Up & Go Online

Register as a driver and start receiving ride or delivery requests.

2. Accept & Navigate

Accept nearby requests. Smart routing guides you efficiently.

3. Earn & Grow

Complete trips and deliveries, earning income and growing your business.
